About
Dirty roads are a data problem.
We made the data visible.

Road cleanliness directly affects public health, stormwater quality, and how communities feel about where they live. Most cleanliness assessments rely on slow, expensive manual surveys.

NeatNet turns any smartphone photograph into a precise cleanliness readout — powered by a computer vision model trained on thousands of road surface images across urban and rural environments.

Why It Matters
🌿

Environmental health

Litter and debris enter waterways during rainfall, harming ecosystems. Identifying problem roads early makes intervention faster and cheaper.

⚡

Instant AI analysis

Our model evaluates texture, object density, and surface anomalies in parallel — returning a confidence-weighted score in under three seconds.

🎯

Three-tier scoring system

Clean, Moderate, and Dirty thresholds are calibrated against municipal cleanliness standards. Each score includes a full confidence breakdown.

🔒

Zero data retained

Your image is analysed in memory and immediately discarded. NeatNet never stores road photographs or location data.
